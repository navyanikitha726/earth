THE JOURNEY OF FIRE ON EARTH

Fire on Earth: a natural phenomenon and a growing concern,requires three essential elements: heat, oxygen, and fuel, commonly referred to as the fire triangle.

Early Earth

For billions of years, life evolved on Earth without encountering fire. Volcanic activity might have produced fire fountains or isolated flames, but these were not true fires in the presence of oxygen.
