Fresh Air

Fresh air is naturally occurring outdoor air, typically considered to be free from pollutants and toxins
It's crucial for human health and well-being, providing oxygen for survival and offering various benefits
like improved mood, energy levels, and immune function. Breathing fresh air can also help clear the lungs
improve digestion, and even contribute to better sleep

Polluted Air

Air pollution is the contamination of the air by harmful substances
including gases, particles, and
biological molecules that can negatively impact human health and the environment, These pollutants can originate from natural sources
like volcanic eruptions and wildfires, as well as human activities such as burning fossil fuels.
